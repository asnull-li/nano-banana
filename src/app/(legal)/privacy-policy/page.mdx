# Privacy Policy for Nano Banana

**Last Updated: September 24, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Nano Banana, the **revolutionary AI-powered image generation and editing platform** that delivers consistent character editing and scene preservation. At Nano Banana, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our AI image editing services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using Nano Banana:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account and provide customer support.

2. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use Nano Banana, including your AI image editing interactions, features accessed, usage frequency, and creative workflows.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement and improve our AI image editing capabilities.

3. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access Nano Banana, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our AI processing services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

4. **Creative Content and Processing Data**

   - **What We Collect**: Images you upload for editing, text prompts you provide, and editing parameters you specify when using Nano Banana's AI features.
   - **Purpose**: To process your requests and generate edited images according to your specifications.
   - **Important Privacy Commitment**: **We do not use your uploaded images or generated images to train our AI models.** Your creative content remains private and is used solely for providing you with the requested editing services.

5. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our AI image editing services and personalize your creative experience.

6. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for Nano Banana subscriptions and credits.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information and creative content seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers with enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, access controls, and secure AI processing environments to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and creative works. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
